about us

MATT BREWBAKER

PRODUCER

Matt Brewbaker recently worked on Disney’s cutting-edge motion-capture project A Christmas Carol starring Jim Carrey, and was an office/editorial assistant, working directly under Academy-Award winning producers, Robert Zemeckis (Back to the Future, Forest Gump) and Steve Starkey (What Lies Beneath, Cast Away).

Matt has worked in development for heavyweight production companies such as The Donner’s Company and New Regency. He’s also worked in production on the following big-budget films: “Hancock”, “First Daughter”, “Imagine That”, and “Beowulf”.

Matt’s production and editorial experience extends into reality television on such network shows as Hell’s Kitchen, I Know My Kid’s a Star, and Age of Love. Matt is currently the chief producer at Viscape Arts, a movie production company formed by TD Ameritrade financial consultants

RICHARD INGBER

PRODUCER

Richard Ingber is an internationally renowned producer known for producing positive, family-oriented dramas such as Dolphin Tale and Dolphin Tale 2.

Ingber, working in conjunction with Warners' marketing department, previously led Alcon's campaigns for such recent releases as the romantic comedy "P.S. I Love You," starring Hilary Swank and Gerard Butler, "One Missed Call," "16 Blocks," "Racing Stripes," "Insomnia" and "My Dog Skip." Ingber, a 20-year marketing and distribution veteran, has worked at Columbia, Fox, Disney and Largo Entertainment.

He formed the marketing consulting company Richard Ingber Entertainment in 1998, working with filmmakers and studios' marketing and distribution divisions to create comprehensive campaigns for titles. The company also developed behind-the- scenes creative content, which included electronic press kits, DVD extra content, viral Internet videos and television specials.

ANTHONY SANDS

WRITER/PRODUCER

Tony Sands is an international award winning writer-director, with his TV family special, You Will See, receiving a Telly Award, for “Best Family TV”, and his previous work, Assumptions, winning the Gabriel Award for “Best Family Drama”.

This and several of his recent TV episodes in the Manifest Mystery series have garnered 11 prizes from such competitions as the Accolades and the New York Festivals. His career in entertainment began over 15 years ago and he has credits on such films as “Titanic”, “Dr. Dolittle” and “Space Jam”.

He currently works as a Senior Producer and Director for Family Theater Productions which for over 65 years has produced quality Film, TV, and Radio for family and faith based audiences. He is also a published author with his literary work available at Barnes & Noble. Previously, he graduated Magna Cum Laude from the USC School of Cinema/Television where he was the recipient of the Stephen Gentry Fellowship.
